The area of ancients called that "Pen Din Samoer" (flat land) is located Moo2 Tambon Khlong Thom Nuea, Amphoe Klongtom, Krabi Province.

In the year 2000 Krabi Coffees were planted cover 4000 Hectare of land. Afterwards Free Trade Area has been established which affect the price of agricultural crops cause of grower reduced their cultivated area of Krabi coffee only 400 Hectare were retained. The group of agriculturist housewife have banded up together to establish a processed coffee group. Under the name of "A fresh coffee processed of agriculturist housewife group of Klongton Nua.
